# GirishOS Avatar Photo

Place your photo here for the avatar. 

## Requirements:
- **Format**: PNG or JPG
- **Size**: At least 512x512 pixels
- **Pose**: Front-facing, looking at camera
- **Expression**: Neutral (slight smile is OK)
- **Lighting**: Good, even lighting on face
- **Background**: Plain/simple background preferred
- **Crop**: Head and shoulders visible

## File naming:
Name your file `girish_photo.png` or `girish_photo.jpg`

## Tips for best results:
- Avoid glasses if possible (can cause artifacts)
- No hat or head covering
- Hair should not cover forehead
- Webcam photo is fine if lighting is good
